Docronym will take the post files' names as post names. It will take your posts and show it. No generation, or anything is needed. You write your post, put it in the posts folder.

Docronym is pretty light

Any JavaScript framework isn't used. So there is no virtual dom, no compiling into JS. The only library that Docronym use is JQuery for fetching your posts. We plan to remove this and find ways to do this in pure JavaScript.
